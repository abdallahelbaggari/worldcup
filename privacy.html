Privacy Policy

Version 1.0 — Last Updated June 11, 2026
WorldCup is an independent fan platform and is not affiliated with, endorsed by, sponsored by, or officially associated with any football governing body, tournament organiser, federation, broadcaster, league, or trademark owner. This Privacy Policy explains how we handle information when you use our application.

1. Who We Are

WorldCup ("the App", "we", "us") is an independent community application that operates within the Pi Network ecosystem. The App is accessible via WorldCup.pi (currently hosted through worldcup-app.pages.dev infrastructure). We are not associated with any official football organisation, governing body, or tournament management entity.

2. Information We Collect

We collect the minimum information necessary to provide the service:

  • Pi Network username: Provided by the Pi Network SDK upon authentication. Used solely to identify your fan profile within the App.
  • App activity data: Predictions you make, points you earn, and preferences you set (language, saved stories). Stored locally within your browser storage mechanisms (including localStorage where applicable).
  • Payment records: Pi payment identifiers (payment IDs and transaction IDs) processed through the Pi Network SDK. We do not store wallet addresses, balances, or any financial data beyond what is required to confirm a transaction was completed.

We do not collect your real name, email address, phone number, physical address, or any biometric data.

3. How We Use Information

By using the App, you consent to the collection and processing of information described in this Privacy Policy solely for operation, authentication, and feature delivery purposes.

  • To authenticate you via the Pi Network and display your fan profile.
  • To process Pi payments for the Fan Pass feature using the Pi Network payment API.
  • To save your preferences on your device (language selection, saved news stories, prediction history).
  • To display leaderboard rankings based on prediction points earned within the App.

We do not use your information for advertising, profiling, or sale to third parties.

4. Pi Network SDK and Payments

This App integrates the Pi Network SDK to enable authentication and Pi cryptocurrency payments. When you sign in or make a payment, the Pi Network SDK processes your credentials and transaction data in accordance with the Pi Network Privacy Policy and the Pi Network Terms of Service. Please review those policies carefully.

Pi payments are processed on the Pi Network Mainnet. All payment approvals and completions are handled via the Pi Network API at api.minepi.com. We receive confirmation that a payment was completed but do not have access to your wallet or its contents. Note that blockchain transaction records are permanent and cannot be modified or deleted.

5. Local Storage

The App stores the following data locally within your browser storage mechanisms (including localStorage where applicable):

  • Your Pi username (session persistence)
  • Language preference
  • Prediction history and earned points
  • Saved news stories
  • Fan Pass activation status

This data resides on your device only and is not transmitted to our servers unless necessary to process a Pi payment.

6. Data Sharing

We do not sell, rent, or trade your information with third parties. We share data only in the following limited circumstances:

  • Pi Network: Payment identifiers are shared with the Pi Network API to approve and complete transactions.
  • Legal requirements: If required by applicable law or a valid legal process.

7. Third-Party Services

The App uses the following third-party services to deliver its features:

  • Pi Network SDK (minepi.com) — Authentication and payments
  • TheSportsDB API (thesportsdb.com) — Match data and fixtures
  • Unsplash (unsplash.com) — Editorial images displayed in the news feed
  • Cloudflare Pages (cloudflare.com) — Application hosting and delivery

Each of these services operates under its own privacy policy. We encourage you to review them independently.

8. Data Retention

Data stored locally on your device persists until you clear your browser storage or uninstall the Pi Browser. You may request deletion of eligible server-side records where legally and technically feasible; however, blockchain transaction records cannot be modified or deleted.

9. Children's Privacy

The App is intended for users who meet the minimum age requirements of the Pi Network as set out in Pi Network's Terms of Service. We do not knowingly collect information from individuals below that age threshold.

10. Your Rights

You may:

  • Clear all locally stored data at any time by clearing your browser's localStorage.
  • Sign out of the App at any time via More → Sign Out.
  • Request deletion of eligible server-side records where legally and technically feasible.

11. Security

We implement industry-standard practices to protect data in transit, including HTTPS encryption for all API communications. We do not store sensitive personal data on our servers.

12. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Material changes will be indicated by a revised version date. Continued use of the App after changes are published constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.

13. Contact

For privacy requests, legal notices, or support inquiries, contact us through More → Support within the App.
