Help Center
🚀 Getting Started
How do I sign in to WorldCup?
Tap "Sign In with Pi" on the login screen. The Pi Network SDK will authenticate you using your Pi Browser credentials. You must have the Pi Browser installed and be logged in to a Pi account.
Is WorldCup free to use?
Yes — WorldCup is completely free. The optional Fan Pass Premium (1π one-time) unlocks additional features including unlimited predictions, all analytics tools, and a verified fan badge.
What is worldcup.pi?
worldcup.pi is WorldCup's official Pi Network domain. The app is also accessible at worldcup-app.pages.dev. It is an independent community fan platform — an independent community fan platform.
🧠 Predictions & Points
How do I earn points?
You earn 5 points for every prediction made, +10 bonus if your prediction is correct, +10 for daily bonus claims, and +5 for every fan zone check-in. Fan Pass Premium gives +50 bonus points on purchase.
How does the streak work?
Your streak increases each day you claim the daily bonus. Longer streaks give higher bonus multipliers. Missing a day resets the streak to zero.
Can I change my prediction?
Yes — tap a different outcome to update your prediction. Once a match starts, predictions are locked for that match. Each match allows one prediction at a time.
💸 Pi Payments
Is this using real Pi?
Yes. WorldCup operates on Pi Network Mainnet (sandbox:false). All Pi transactions — including Fan Pass Premium (1π) and Send Pi — use real Pi. Always confirm the amount before approving.
What does Fan Pass Premium include?
Fan Pass Premium (1π one-time) includes: unlimited predictions, all analytics tools unlocked, group simulator, team comparison, verified fan badge on your Fan ID, and +50 bonus points.
My payment says "Payment Expired" — what do I do?
This can happen due to network delays. If Pi was deducted but your Fan Pass isn't active, contact support with your Pi transaction ID at support.worldcup@gmail.com and we'll resolve it manually.
🎟️ Tickets & QR
What are QR digital tickets?
QR digital tickets are community fan tokens — digital collectibles tied to match events. They are NOT real stadium entry tickets and do not grant physical venue access. They are for fan community use only.
My Fan ID QR is not showing
Ensure you are signed in with Pi. Go to More → Fan ID Card. If the QR is blank, tap "Refresh Fan ID". If the issue persists, check that your browser supports canvas elements.
📊 Live Data
Why is live data delayed?
Live match data updates every 15–30 seconds depending on match status. Data comes from TheSportsDB API. If the API is temporarily unavailable, the app shows cached data with a yellow status badge.
I see wrong match scores
Data is sourced from TheSportsDB public API. If scores appear incorrect, use the refresh button at the top of the Matches screen. For persistent errors, report via More → Report Issue.
