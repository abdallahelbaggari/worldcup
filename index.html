Privacy Policy
Data we collect: Pi Network username only (via Pi SDK). All app activity (predictions, points, check-ins) stored locally on your device.

Pi payments: Processed by Pi Network. We receive only payment confirmation IDs — never wallet balances or private keys.

No sharing: We do not sell or share your data with third parties.

Local storage: All data stored under prefix "wc2_" in your browser. Never leaves your device.
